SAIR

Welcome to the SAIR project website. Here you can find information about our project, its advantages, and how to get in touch with us.

Product

SAIR (Smart Accident Incident Recognition) is an innovative AI-powered system designed to detect and report traffic accidents in real-time. By analyzing live camera feeds using computer vision technology, SAIR can automatically identify accident occurrences without human supervision.

The system aims to improve emergency response times, reduce traffic congestion, and enhance overall road safety by providing instant alerts to relevant authorities. It can be deployed directly on smart road cameras or integrated with existing monitoring systems.

Advantages

Real-time Accident Detection
SAIR detects accidents the moment they occur through live camera feeds.

SAIR uses AI to detect accidents in real-time and alert authorities faster, improving response time.

High Accuracy
Uses AI models trained on diverse accident datasets for precise detection.

It reduces traffic congestion by identifying incident zones and rerouting nearby vehicles automatically.

Easy Integration
Designed for compatibility with existing smart camera networks.

Helps save lives through early accident detection using smart cameras and AI-powered analytics.
